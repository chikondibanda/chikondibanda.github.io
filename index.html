About Me

Professional Profile

Ever since I built my first website in high school, I've been fascinated by the intersection of technology and human impact. I'm Chikondi — a Platform Specialist, self-taught builder, and digital strategist based in Lilongwe, Malawi.

My journey has taken me across fintech, media, social enterprise, and advocacy — from co-founding a tech-for-good organization to leading digital strategy for a UN Foundation-affiliated girls' empowerment program. Along the way, I've picked up PHP, Go, Python, cloud infrastructure, and more — not for the sake of the tools, but for what they can build.

I speak three languages (English, Chichewa, and Japanese — mada mada but I'm learning), and I believe technology should serve people, not the other way around. Whether I'm automating a workflow, designing a brand identity, or mentoring the next generation of African tech talent, I bring the same philosophy: build thoughtfully, iterate relentlessly, and never lose sight of who you're building for.

When I'm not behind a screen, you'll find me creating content for my YouTube channel, exploring Lilongwe's growing tech scene, or learning something new just because it sparks my curiosity. Let's build something meaningful.
