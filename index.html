About Me

I have been a graphic designer and web developer for the past 5 years. During this time, I have had the priviledge to work with wonderful human beings and created amazing projects.

Some of the projects I worked on include converting a figma design into a WordPress site for Greendawn, an EV company based in the UK and developing a web based voting system for St. John Paul II college with my brother, Sam.

Along the way, I have also created social media graphics, designed company profiles and managed social media accounts. I worked in multiple industries including banking, technology and entertainment.

In my free time, I love reading physical books, sketching and making content.
